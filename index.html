Hi. My name is Fengyue(Lisa) Zhao. I am a first-year PhD student at the Department of Linguistics, Cornell University. My research interests lie in computational linguistics, phonetics, and psycholinguistics. I am part of the Cornell Phonetics Lab and the Computational Psycholinguistics Discussions research group .

I got my BA in Linguistics and BS in Mathematics with a minor in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. My undergraduate honors thesis, advised by Dr. Brian Dillon and Dr. Ming Xiang , was on modeling pronoun ambiguity resolution using bayesian models.
