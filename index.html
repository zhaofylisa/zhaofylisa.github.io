Hi. My name is Fengyue(Lisa) Zhao. I am a second-year PhD student at the Department of Linguistics, Cornell University. My research interests lie in phonetics, phonology, computational linguistics, and psycholinguistics. I am part of the Cornell Phonetics Lab and the Computational Psycholinguistics Discussions research group . I got my B.A. in Linguistics and B.S. in Mathematics with a minor in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst.
