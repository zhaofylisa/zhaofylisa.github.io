Excited to share that our poster, **“Laryngeal elevation in native and non-native lexical tone production,”** will be presented at two upcoming conferences this summer:

📍 **190th Meeting of the Acoustical Society of America (ASA)** |
May 11–15, 2026 | Philadelphia, PA, USA

📍 **Laboratory Phonology 20 (LabPhon 20)** |
June 26–28, 2026 | Montréal, Québec, Canada

Co-authored with Sam Tilsen and Mark Tiede, this work explores how laryngeal elevation patterns differ between native and non-native speakers in lexical tone production, using coronal-oriented laryngeal ultrasound imaging.
